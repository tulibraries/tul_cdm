Rosenbluth Brothers

History of Rosenbluth Brothers

The company was founded in 1892 by Marcus Rosenbluth and initially sold passage between New York and Philadelphia before expanding to include corporate and leisure travel arrangements.


To learn more about our collection of steamship ticket purchase ledgers and the history of immigrant banking in America, view the Steamship agents page.


Rosenbluth ledger

There is one ledger containing ticket purchases made at Rosenbluth Brothers between 1907 and 1910. The ledger contains a handwritten alphabetical index of passenger names corresponding to specific entries within the ledger.
